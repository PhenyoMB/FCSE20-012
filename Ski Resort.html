Welcome to Ounasvaara! Ounasvaara is a family ski resort close by the city of Rovaniemi at the Arctic Circle. Besides the downhill skiing slopes there are also several maintained cross-country skiing tracks in the area.
In the summer time you can enjoy the speed by summer-tobogganing or by down-hill biking. From the top of Ounasvaara hill there is a beautiful landscape to be seen throughout the year.

About Ounasvaara Ski Resort:

Ounasvaara Ski Resort has got ten slopes – the longest track 600 meters in length. Ounasvaara hill HD is 140 meters. There are five lifts and a Street with many rails and boxes for both beginners and advanced downhill skiers there. Rendi´s Snow World is perfect training place for children and beginners.
Ounasvaara has been the breeding ground and the home hill for example for world-famous alpinist Tanja Poutiainen and snowboarder Antti Autti.

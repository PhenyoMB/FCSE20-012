Santasport pool area is ideal for competitive swimmers, aquatics, swim schools, and recreational swimming. After swimming, you can relax in the warmth of the hot tub or enjoy the bubbles in the Jacuzzi. Every year we host several inspiring campaigns, events, and relaxing candle swimming in intimate candle lighting. Our pool area is a big open space where it is easy for the entire family to enjoy swimming together. For children we have a small pool with a slide and a collection of toys to play with in the water.

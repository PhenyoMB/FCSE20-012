In a traditional rustic setting at the Arctic Circle, next door to Santa Claus, you’ll find a welcoming little farmyard with reindeer, alpacas, sheep, goats, peacocks, rabbits, chicks, hens and roosters. You can watch domestic animals in their daily activities under Arctic weather conditions and, best of all, you can pet and feed most of them. Kids love them and they love kids!
Cross-country skiing: In winter time, Elf’s Farmyard offers an opportunity to cross the Arctic Circle on traditional Lappish wooden skis. And there’s more winter fun –coasting down the little hill on site! All the equipment you need is provided.
Guests can use our Lapland hut (kota) and campfire/barbecue. Bring your own food or buy snacks and drinks from the elf’s store.
All this is included in the entrance fee. Elf’s Farmyard is located at the Arctic Circle, at the north end of Santa Claus Village – and quite near Santa Claus himself. Visitors warmly welcome!
